# Code of Conduct
- Our Pledge: We pledge to act in ways that contribute to a positive environment.
- Our Standards: Examples of behavior include being welcoming and respectful.
- Enforcement Responsibilities: Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards.
- Scope: This applies to all project spaces.
- Enforcement: Instances of abusive behavior may be reported.
- Enforcement Guidelines: Maintainers will follow these guidelines to determine consequences.
- Attribution: Adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
